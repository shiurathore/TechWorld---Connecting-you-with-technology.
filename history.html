HISTORY

Discover the Past, Inspire the Future

Take a journey through the milestones of technology. From the invention of the first computers to the rise of the internet and AI, explore how innovations of the past shaped the digital world we live in today.

Ancient Innovations

From the invention of the wheel (~3500 BC) to the Antikythera mechanism, ancient civilizations laid the foundation for modern technology.

Industrial Revolution (18th–19th Century)

Steam engines, mechanized factories, and electricity transformed human life, leading to mass production and urbanization.

Computing Era (20th Century)

The development of ENIAC, microprocessors, and personal computers revolutionized industries, communication, and everyday life.

Digital & Internet Age

The rise of the Internet, smartphones, and social media connected the world and shaped the modern digital society.

Artificial Intelligence & Future

AI, robotics, biotechnology, and quantum computing are shaping the future, pushing the boundaries of human capabilities.
